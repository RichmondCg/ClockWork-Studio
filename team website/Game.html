Memory Game

It is easy to play. You just need to guess and click the cards. Every card has its pair and when a pair of cards has picked it will vanish, continuesly until you win the game. Modes and Levels

There are two(2) modes of the game. The Main game mode and the Relax game mode.

In Main game mode there are five(5) levels. From easy to hard yet challenging. It has a timer from level 1 continuesly running until the last level(5). It also has a dictated limit of move you can made every level.

Level 1 - You only have 4 cards to guess.

Level 2 - You will have 10 cards to guess.

Level 3 - You will have 14 cards to guess.

Level 4 - In this level the game will be challenging. It will give you 25 cards to guess.

Level 5(final) - In this final stage of the game, the most quite challenging. You will have 60 cards to guess.
